About Bukhara

Located at ITC Maurya, New Delhi, Bukhara is a world-famous North Indian restaurant known for its authentic tandoor cooking, rustic ambiance, and iconic dishes like Dal Bukhara and Sikandari Raan. A destination for diplomats, global travelers, and food connoisseurs.
